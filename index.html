How Do You Know if Water Damage is Permanent?

Water damage can have lasting effects on your property, but how can you tell if it’s permanent? When faced with water damage, it’s important to assess the severity of the situation to determine the necessary steps for restoration.

Key Takeaways:

  • Water damage can lead to mold growth, damage to floors, walls, and electrics, and even structural issues if not addressed promptly.
  • Signs of water damage include bulging and warping of personal belongings, soaking of soft furnishings, peeling paint or wallpaper, and the development of mold.
  • Taking immediate action when water damage occurs, such as removing pets and children from the affected area, containing the leak, and contacting an insurance company, is crucial.
  • Hiring a professional water damage restoration service can help assess the extent of the damage and perform necessary repairs.
  • Drying out the affected areas using methods like air drying, high-powered fans, dehumidifiers, and carpet cleaning machines is essential in the restoration process.
  • The time required for water damage repairs depends on factors such as the extent of the damage and the size of the affected area.
